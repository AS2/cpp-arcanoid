1) level legend
- first string: two numbers - width and height
- * - no block
- 1 - block for 1 hit
- 2 - block for 2 hit
- 3 - block for 3 hit
bonuses (all have 4 hits to break):
- T - make platform tiny
- B - make platform big
- L - gives extra live
- H - hard ball
- M - multiballs
- P - points (+1000 points)
- F - make ball faster
- S - make ball slower
